Student declaration has not been accepted

What happens next

Please speak to your Disability Advisor or HEI team to let them know that you have not accepted the student declaration. They can talk you through the statements on the page, review the application with you and make any relevant changes. Once they have done this, you will get another notification to sign in, accept your student declaration, and submit the application for processing.

We will automatically withdraw this application if no action is taken within 90 days.

If you need help with anything else, please contact the DSA Team.
