Only fill out the forms on this page if you have been asked to do so by SAAS or your Disability Advisor. If you do not see the form you need, please contact us.

Before you complete a form:

  1. Download and install Adobe Acrobat on your device. The reader version is free of charge. Do not use third party PDF software as it may not work correctly.
  2. Choose the form you need and save a copy. Do not complete the form in your browser window.
  3. Save a copy on your device and fill in the form. Make sure you sign and date the form, you can type your name in the signature box.
  4. Upload the form to your SAAS Account using the Document Uploader.
