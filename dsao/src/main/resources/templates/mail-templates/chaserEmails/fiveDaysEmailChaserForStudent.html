What happens next

Please click on the link in this email to review the information your Disability Advisor has provided to make it is correct. This should only take a few minutes.

Once you're happy with the application information, you will need to click 'I agree' on the student declaration page and confirm the bank details where you want the funds to be paid. You can enter a different bank account from the one we pay your main SAAS funding into so that you can keep the DSA money separate.

If anything on the application is incorrect or you do not understand any of the statements on the student declaration, please click on ‘I do not agree’ on the student declaration page and contact your Disability Advisor to discuss the next steps.

It is important that you click either ‘I agree’ or ‘I do not agree’ otherwise your Disability Advisor cannot make changes and your application will not be processed.

Getting help

It’s important that you check the details on your DSA application and accept the student declaration to avoid any delays in getting your funding. If you are unsure of what to do next or think something on the application is incorrect, please talk to your Disability Advisor who will be able to help.

Keeping your account secure

Never share your sign in details with anyone. Remember to keep your account information, including your email address, up-to-date.
