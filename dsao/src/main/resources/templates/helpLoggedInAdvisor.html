These forms are for students to complete and submit to SAAS. They will need to:

  1. Download and install Adobe Acrobat on their device. The reader version is free of charge, they must not use third party PDF software as it may not work correctly.
  2. Choose the relevant form and save a copy. They must not complete the form in a browser window.
  3. Save a copy on their device and fill in the form. They must sign and date the form, and they can type their name in the signature box.
  4. Upload the form to their SAAS Account using the Document Uploader.
