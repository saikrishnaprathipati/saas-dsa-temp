Payments

Your DSA funding will be paid into the bank account you gave us when you submitted your application. If your course has started, we will pay this into your bank account within the next 3 to 5 working days. Otherwise, we will make the payment within 2 weeks of your course start date.


Purchasing items

To learn more about purchasing items, such as upgrading an electrical or ergonomic item, purchasing something different from what your Disability Advisor has recommended, or sourcing an item from a different supplier, read our DSA Guidance Notes.


Receipts

When you purchase the items outlined in this award, make sure you keep your receipts, as you will need to send these to us using the Document Uploader in your SAAS Account within 6 weeks of purchasing the items. Read our DSA Online Application Guide for details on how to do this.


If anything changes

If you no longer need DSA, or you need additional support, contact your Disability Advisor.


Getting help

If you need further help in understanding this award, purchasing your recommended equipment, organising your travel arrangements or your Non-Medical Personal Help, contact your Disability Advisor. If you need help uploading receipts or signing into your SAAS Account, contact us.

We recommend you keep a copy of this award notice for your records.
