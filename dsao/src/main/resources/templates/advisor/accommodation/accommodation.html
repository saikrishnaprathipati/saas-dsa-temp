DSA does not cover the cost of student accommodation. However, we can pay the difference if the student needs enhanced accommodation because of a disability when living in private accommodation.

If the student is living in institution-run accommodation, the college or university should consider their duties under the Equality Act in regards to reasonable adjustments and consider whether the additional costs should be met by their own budgets.

Details of the accommodation requirement must be included in the Needs Assessment Report.
