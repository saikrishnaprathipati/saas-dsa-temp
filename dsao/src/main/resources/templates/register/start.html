Create your Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) Account

The DSA online application service lets you populate a DSA application on behalf of a student without the need to upload a Needs Assessment Report or medical evidence. Once you have submitted a completed application, the student will receive an email asking them to sign in to their SAAS Account to check the details you have provided and submit it to SAAS for processing.

To use this service you will need to create a DSA Account. This lets you sign in at any time, and save an application so that you can come back to the service when needed. It also helps us to keep the information you give us secure.

To create a DSA Account you will need an active email address associated with your college or university.
